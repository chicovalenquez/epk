THE STORY
The hockey-playing, donut-eating landlocked surf three-piece from Saskatchewan released a handful of music early in the 2000s, but failed to maintain momentum between bouts in jail and rehab.
After a moment of clarity during lockdown, The Saskatones reformed with a an entirely new rhythm section in the equally surfless plains of Ontario, with a newfound purpose to document our solar system via surf rock.
The "Planets" series launches in 2024.

THE PLANETS
The Saskatones will release an EP for every other planet in our solar system starting with Mercury.
The length of each EP will be logarithmically proportional to the average radius of that planet relative to Earth, which will be assigned a standard value of 10 minutes.
The music will still be surf with a modern edge, but will try to capture the essence of the planet it represents.
