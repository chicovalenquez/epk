ELECTRONIC PRESS KIT
THE STORY
The hockey-playing, donut-eating landlocked surf fanatics from Saskatchewan release a handful of music early in the 2000s, but failed to maintain momentum between bouts in jail and rehab.
After a moment of clarity during lockdown, The Saskatones reformed with a an entirely new rhythm section in the equally surfless plains of Ontario with a newfound purpose to document our solar system via surf rock.
The "Planets" series launches in 2024.

ALTERNATE REALITY
Three piece surf rock currently out of Kitchener-Waterloo, Ontario.
Edgier and weirder than a lot of surf, but still with a healthy dose of nostalgia.
Features veteran musicians formerly members of Bocce, and Knock Knock Ginger.
