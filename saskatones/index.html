SLIGHTLY LONGER BIO
The hockey-playing, donut-eating landlocked surf three-piece from Saskatchewan released a handful of music early in the 2000s, but failed to maintain momentum, allegedy between such distractions as jail and rehab.

Following a moment of clarity during lockdown, lead guitarist Goose Dupond relocated cross country on the search for a new rhythm section. After securing ringers from the Dadmobile house band to round out the lineup, the Saskatones returned with a newfound purpose: To document the planets of our solar system via surf rock!
THE PLANETS
The Saskatones will over time release an EP for every planet in our solar system (other than Earth) starting with Mercury. The length of each EP will be logarithmically proportional to the average radius of that planet relative to Earth, which will be assigned a default value of 10 minutes.

Behold: Science!
