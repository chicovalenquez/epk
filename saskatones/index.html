SLIGHTLY LONGER BIO
The hockey-playing, donut-eating landlocked surf three-piece from Saskatchewan released a handful of music early in the 2000s. Their live energy and odd nerd-punk vibe turned heads but the band failed to maintain momentum thanks to distractions including:
unrealistic hockey dreams, rehab and jail.

Following a moment of clarity during COVID lockdown, lead guitarist Goose Dupond relocated cross country on the search for a new rhythm section. After securing a team of ringers from other Dadmobile bands, the Saskatones have returned with a newfound purpose:
To document our solar system via surf rock!

THE PLANETS SERIES
The Saskatones will gradually release an EP for every planet in our solar system (other than Earth) starting with Mercury. The songs will explore humanity's exploration and understanding of these alien landscapes.

The length of each EP will be logarithmically proportional to the average radius of that planet relative to Earth, which will be assigned a default value of 10 minutes. The following spreadsheet will clarify:
