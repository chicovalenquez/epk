THE STORY
The hockey-playing, donut-eating landlocked surf three-piece from Saskatchewan released a handful of music early in the 2000s, but failed to maintain momentum, allegedy between bouts in jail and rehab.
After a moment of clarity during lockdown, The Saskatones reformed with a an entirely new rhythm section in the equally surfless plains of Ontario. Their newfound purpose is to document the planets of our solar system via surf rock.

Actual band photo
THE QUEST
The Saskatones will release an EP for every planet in our solar system (other than Earth) starting with Mercury.
The length of each EP will be logarithmically proportional to the average radius of that planet relative to Earth, which will be assigned a standard value of 10 minutes.
The music will mostly stick to an instrumental space surf style, but will expand to try to capture the essence of the planet it represents.
